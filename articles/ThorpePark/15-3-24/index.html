Park Review

Thorpe Park VIP Preview event

Cal

Executive

March 15, 2024

During our exclusive visit to the Thorpe Park VIP Preview event, we made the most of our time by capturing glimpses of the park’s various sections giving you a preview of the exciting changes set to debut this season. So, without further ado, let’s delve into the highlights:

Our journey commenced at the main entrance, where a sleek new sign proudly displayed the updated logo amidst fresh paintwork, setting the stage for the forthcoming excitement. Inside the dome, the coffee shack underwent a rejuvenation, presenting a refreshed ambiance to greet eager visitors.

We then headed over to Tidal Wave, where the exit bridge underwent a transformation, reverting to its original Pier 13 branding, evoking nostalgic charm.

Continuing our exploration, we arrived at the transformed “Big Easy Boulevard,” formerly known as “Angry Birds Land.” The metamorphosis here was truly remarkable! The Dodgems received a thematic makeover as “Big Easy Dodgems,” while Detonator also underwent a thematic refresh. Notably, the seamlessly integrated queue boards added an immersive touch to the thematic experience. Additionally, the 4D cinema was reimagined as the “Sunset Cinema,” featuring the captivating movie “Ready Player One,” condensed into a thrilling 15-minute spectacle.

We then headed over to Stealth’s plaza, where extensive renovations took place, including a fresh coat of paint across most of the area and new signage, now showcasing the correct acceleration (0-80 in 1.8s) above the entrance, revitalizing the area with renewed vibrancy.

Samurai returned to its original Chessington colour scheme, delighting many with its timeless elegance. Vortex also underwent a striking transformation with its newly applied coat of paint, exuding a renewed sense of magnificence.

Finally, at Colossus, significant refurbishments were undertaken, including the meticulous repainting of most of the track and thematic elements surrounding the station. It’s worth noting that the park has plans to enhance the very yellow entrance with additional colour later in the season.

But that’s not all! There were multiple changes to some the park’s restaurants, shops and toilets which all look amazing!
